I The Membrane — Integration & Leak 🜄 ∫ capacitor
Neural Model
Passive membrane
Lipid bilayer with embedded ion channels. Charge accumulates across the membrane. Resistance (leak) and capacitance (storage) are biological, not metaphorical.
Circuit Implementation
R_leak × C_mem
220kΩ leak resistor + 10µF electrolytic capacitor. τ = RC ≈ 2.2s. Voltage across C is V_mem. Leak bleeds charge to ground continuously.
Formal Math
τ · dV/dt = −V + R·I(t)
The membrane potential V decays exponentially toward rest (−V/τ) while input current I charges it. τ = RC is the time constant. At equilibrium dV/dt = 0 → V = R·I.

II The Threshold — Comparator & Spike 🜂 δ LM393
Neural Model
Action potential initiation
When V_mem reaches ~−55mV (threshold), voltage-gated Na⁺ channels open. Positive feedback — depolarization opens more channels. All-or-nothing event.
Circuit Implementation
LM393 open-collector
V_mem → IN+ (pin 3). V_thresh → IN− (pin 2) via 10kΩ divider. When V_mem ≥ V_thresh, output pulls LOW (open collector fires). 470Ω pullup to reach ~2.4V sufficient to drive downstream neurons.
Formal Math
V_mem(t) ≥ V_th → spike
The Heaviside step function H(V−V_th). Zero below threshold, one above. The spike itself is modeled as δ(t−t_i) — a Dirac delta, infinitely brief, infinitely intense at moment t_i.

Neural Model
Refractory period
After firing, Na⁺ channels inactivate, K⁺ channels open. Membrane hyperpolarizes below rest (~−70mV). Neuron cannot fire again for ~1–2ms absolute refractory period.
Circuit Implementation
Reset feedback R
100k–220kΩ from LM393 output back to membrane node. Drains capacitor after spike. Tunable — lower R = faster reset = higher max firing rate. No self-oscillation (avoid direct positive feedback loop).
Formal Math
V → V_reset after t_i
Instantaneous reset: V_mem(t_i⁺) = V_r. During absolute refractory τ_ref, neuron is unresponsive regardless of input. Modeled as H(t−t_i−τ_ref) gate on input.

IV Synapses — Weight, Plasticity & STDP 🜍 Δw ∂
Neural Model
Synaptic weight
The strength of connection between two neurons. Determined by receptor density, neurotransmitter release probability, synapse morphology. Not fixed — plastic.
Circuit Implementation
Multi-substrate
Sensory: Ag-agar ECL memristors (variable, biological). Association: alumina/graphite DES paste (reproducible). Output: MCP4131 digital potentiometers (deterministic). Three materials, three regimes.
Formal Math
I_syn = w · g(t) · (V_syn − V)
Synaptic current. w is weight, g(t) is conductance time course (exponential decay after spike), V_syn is reversal potential (positive = excitatory, negative = inhibitory).

Neural Model
STDP — Hebbian plasticity
Spike-timing dependent plasticity. If pre fires before post (causal), synapse strengthens (LTP). If post fires before pre (anti-causal), synapse weakens (LTD). Time window ~20ms biological.
Circuit Implementation
DIY electrochemical
Pre-synaptic RC trace + post-synaptic RC trace. Correlation detected via analog multiplication. Weight update drives memristive element — silver migration in agar (sensory) or alumina reduction (association).
Formal Math
Δw = A₊·e^(−Δt/τ₊) if Δt>0
Δw = −A₋·e^(Δt/τ₋) if Δt<0
Δt = t_post − t_pre. Positive Δt (pre before post) → weight increase. Negative Δt → weight decrease. Exponential decay of effect with temporal distance.

ARCHITECTURE — THE MINI-STACK MODEL 🧠 ≡ ⬡ ≡ 板

The long-term build doesn't scale by cramming more neurons onto one board — it scales by mimicking how the brain actually organizes itself: functional regions, each with its own character, connected by long-range spike pathways.

Each mini-stack is a small cluster of neurons whose R and C values are chosen to match a neural role, not just a circuit role. Fast τ neurons (47kΩ×10µF, ~0.47s) are sensory — they respond to rate, forget quickly, stay at the edge. Slow τ neurons (220kΩ×47µF, ~10s) are integrators — they accumulate evidence across time, hold context, smooth noise. The resistor and capacitor are not just components. They are the cell's personality.

A sensory stack runs noisy MCP6002 op-amps — probabilistic, variable, biologically honest. An association stack runs OPA2340 with fake memristors — reproducible, trainable. An output stack runs LT1013 precision — deterministic, clean, digital-facing. The gradient from chaos to order is built into the material choices, not programmed in software.

Each stack lives on its own board — eventually a bismuth-poured wooden PCB, one artifact per brain area. The boards connect via spike bus wires: visible cables carrying binary spike signals between regions, anatomically equivalent to white matter tracts. The cable is not infrastructure. It is anatomy.

The roadmap in neuron count: Season 1 (~28 neurons, reflex arc) → Season 2 (~70 neurons, basic adaptive behavior) → Season 3 (~150 neurons, clustered stacks, genuine emergence). C. elegans navigates, learns, and avoids predators with 302 neurons. The destination is somewhere in that neighborhood — built from salvaged resistors, recycled boards, and electrochemistry figured out in real time.
